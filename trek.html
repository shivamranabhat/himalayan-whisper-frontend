Annapurna Circuit Trek: A Journey Through the Himalayas

$305 $375

Location

Pokhara

Distance

200+ KM

Duration

4 Nights 5 Days

Level

Hard

Sitting on the spine of the world, you’ll find a humble country named Nepal

While many dreamers and thrill seekers journey to these hills to accomplish an epic feat, there’s a lot more to Nepal than its trails. Explore the country’s sense of spirituality as you visit temples, shrines and pagodas. Enjoy the chaotic hum of Kathmandu then set out on a jeep jungle safari. Learn about local communities while making momos in the mountains. And if you’re here to conquer Everest Base Camp, Annapurna or the Langtang Valley trek, remember to take a break from looking up and look around – after all, it’s about the journey, not the destination.

Highlights
  • Riding & Hiking the TILICHO LAKE Highest Lake in the WORLD at an altitude of 4920m,
  • Pine forest, Apple orchards, Arid Tibetan plateaus in the lower Mustang region,
  • Temples, monasteries and the opportunity to talk to Buddhist monks,
  • The amazing Lubra single trial downhill with back drop of 8000M peaks,
  • Waterfalls, wildlife, yaks, monkeys and Blue sheep. Bring binoculars!
  • Camping out in the open skies surrounded by the Himalayas
  • Gangapurna Glacier Lake,
  • Apple pies, wine and brandy,
  • Activities in Pokhara: Biking to a local natural swimming hole, Boating and sightseeing to World Peace Pagoda and Devisfall.
Itinerary
Day 1. Kathmandu

Not many groups stop in Phortse – a traditional Sherpa village that offers an insight into their life and culture – so today will be something special. Leave Namche and weave your way above the valley, then take the less-travelled fork in the road just after Sanasa. Most travellers follow the same path to and from Base Camp, while you’ll make more of a loop to see more of the national park. Climb for some epic views of the valley below, and then stop for a mini acclimatisation lunch at around 4000 m.
